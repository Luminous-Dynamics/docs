## How to Participate

### Starting a Discussion

1. **Go to** [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/Luminous-Dynamics/discussions)
2. **Click** "New discussion"
3. **Choose** appropriate category
4. **Write** a clear title and description
5. **Add** any relevant tags
6. **Post** and engage with responses

**Tips for good discussions:**

- ‚úÖ **Search first** - Your question might be answered
- ‚úÖ **Clear title** - "How to X?" not "Help!"
- ‚úÖ **Provide context** - Version, OS, what you tried
- ‚úÖ **One topic** - Keep discussions focused
- ‚úÖ **Be respectful** - Follow [Code of Conduct](code-of-conduct.md)

### Responding to Discussions

**Good responses:**

- ‚úÖ **Helpful and constructive**
- ‚úÖ **Include examples or links**
- ‚úÖ **Ask clarifying questions**
- ‚úÖ **Share your experience**
- ‚úÖ **Upvote helpful answers**

**Avoid:**

- ‚ùå "Just Google it" (if you know the answer, share it)
- ‚ùå Off-topic tangents
- ‚ùå Arguments or personal attacks
- ‚ùå Spam or self-promotion
- ‚ùå "Necroposting" unless adding value

## Community Guidelines

### Be Welcoming

- **No "RTFM"** - If docs answer it, link kindly
- **No "just"** - "Just do X" makes it sound trivial
- **Assume good intent** - Give people benefit of doubt
- **Welcome questions** - Everyone was a beginner once
- **Celebrate learning** - Progress deserves recognition

### Be Helpful

- **Share knowledge** - Don't gatekeep
- **Provide context** - Explain the "why" not just "what"
- **Link to resources** - Help people learn more
- **Update when solved** - Help future searchers
- **Thank helpers** - Gratitude goes a long way

### Be Respectful

- **Follow [Code of Conduct](code-of-conduct.md)**
- **Disagree kindly** - Focus on ideas, not people
- **Stay on topic** - Keep discussions focused
- **Respect time** - Be concise and clear
- **Assume positive intent** - People are trying to help

## Moderation

### What Gets Moderated

We will remove:

- ‚ùå **Spam or advertising** (unless clearly beneficial to community)
- ‚ùå **Harassment or hate speech** (zero tolerance)
- ‚ùå **Off-topic discussions** (we'll suggest moving elsewhere)
- ‚ùå **Duplicate questions** (we'll link to original)
- ‚ùå **Code of Conduct violations**

### How We Moderate

1. **Warning** - First offense, gentle redirect
2. **Edit/Hide** - Remove problematic content
3. **Lock** - Prevent further comments if heated
4. **Temporary ban** - Cooling-off period
5. **Permanent ban** - Repeated or severe violations

**Appeal:** Contact conduct@luminousdynamics.org

### Self-Moderation

Help us keep discussions healthy:

- üö© **Report issues** - Use GitHub's report feature
- üëç **Upvote quality** - Signal what's helpful
- üìù **Edit your posts** - Fix mistakes, add updates
- ü§ù **De-escalate** - If things get heated, step back

## Tips for Great Discussions

### Ask Great Questions

**Format:**

```markdown
# Clear, specific title

## What I'm trying to do
[Describe your goal]

## What I tried
[Show what you attempted]

## What happened
[Actual result]

## What I expected
[Expected result]

## System info
- Luminous Nix version: 1.0.0
- NixOS version: 23.11
- Other relevant info

## Additional context
[Anything else relevant]
```

### Give Great Answers

**Format:**

```markdown
## Here's how to solve this:

[Clear explanation]

```code
[Working example]
```

## Why this works:
[Explanation of the solution]

## Learn more:
- [Link to docs]
- [Link to related discussion]
```

### Write Great Show and Tell

**Format:**

```markdown
# [Catchy title of what you built]

## What I built
[Description and demo/screenshots]

## Why I built it
[The problem or opportunity]

## How I built it
[Process and tools used]

## What I learned
[Key insights and challenges]

## What's next
[Future plans]

## Resources
- [Links to code, docs, etc.]

Happy to answer questions!
```

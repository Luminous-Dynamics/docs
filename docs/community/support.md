### Built-in Help

For Luminous Nix users:

```bash
# Ask Luminous Nix itself!
luminous-nix ask "how do I [your question]?"

# Show all available commands
luminous-nix --help

# Get help on specific features
luminous-nix help voice
luminous-nix help configuration
```

### What to Include in Support Requests

Help us help you faster by including:

**For Luminous Nix issues:**

```
- Luminous Nix version: (run `luminous-nix --version`)
- NixOS version: (run `nixos-version`)
- What you tried to do:
- What actually happened:
- Error messages (if any):
- Steps to reproduce:
```

**For Terra Lumina issues:**

```
- Your account email:
- What you were trying to do:
- What actually happened:
- Screenshots (if relevant):
- Browser and OS:
```

**General Tips:**

- Be specific: "Can't install packages" vs "Getting error XYZ when running command ABC"
- Include screenshots/logs when relevant
- Mention what you've already tried
- One issue per email (multiple issues = multiple emails)

## Security Vulnerabilities

**Found a security issue? Please report responsibly.**

!!! danger "DO NOT report security issues publicly"
    Do not open public GitHub issues or discuss in Discord/forums.

**Report privately to**: security@luminousdynamics.org

**Include:**

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Any suggested fixes (if you have them)

**We commit to:**

- Acknowledge your report within 48 hours
- Provide a timeline for fixing within 7 days
- Credit you in the security advisory (unless you prefer anonymity)
- Keep you updated throughout the process

**Our security policy**: [github.com/Luminous-Dynamics/luminous-nix/security/policy](https://github.com/Luminous-Dynamics/luminous-nix/security/policy)

## Support Hours & Expectations

### Community Channels (Discord, Discussions)

- **24/7 availability**: Community members from around the world
- **Response time**: Varies, no guarantees
- **Best for**: Non-urgent questions, learning, connection

### Email Support

- **Business hours**: Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm PT
- **Response time**: 1-3 business days for initial response
- **Resolution time**: Depends on complexity
- **Holidays**: Reduced support during major holidays

### Emergency Support

We don't currently offer 24/7 emergency support for free users. Enterprise customers can purchase SLA-backed support.

**If you have a critical production issue:**

1. Email support@luminousdynamics.org with "URGENT" in subject
2. Include detailed reproduction steps
3. Post in Discord for community assistance while waiting

## Frequently Asked About Support

### Can I pay for faster support?

Yes! Enterprise support with SLAs is available. Contact: enterprise@luminousdynamics.org

### Do you offer phone support?

Not currently for individual users. Enterprise customers can arrange phone/video support.

### What languages do you support?

Currently English only for direct support. Community members may help in other languages via Discord.

### Can you help me with general NixOS questions unrelated to Luminous Nix?

We focus on Luminous Nix support, but the community often helps with broader NixOS questions. For general NixOS help, try:

- [NixOS Discourse](https://discourse.nixos.org/)
- [NixOS Matrix chat](https://matrix.to/#/#community:nixos.org)
- [r/NixOS on Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/NixOS/)

### I sent an email but haven't heard back

Check your spam folder first. If it's been >3 business days, send a follow-up or ping us in Discord.

### Can maintainers help via DM/email for personal projects?

Please use public channels (Discord, Discussions) rather than DMing maintainers. This:

- Helps others who have similar questions
- Gets you faster answers (more people can help)
- Builds community knowledge base
- Respects maintainers' time

We reserve DMs for private issues (security, personal info, etc.).

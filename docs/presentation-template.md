## Presentation Tips

### Delivery Advice

**Timing:**
- Quick pitch: 1 min per slide, 5-8 slides
- Standard: 1-2 min per slide, 12-15 slides
- Deep dive: 2-3 min per slide, all slides

**Engagement:**
- Start with a question
- Show actual demos when possible
- Share personal experience
- Invite questions throughout

**Adaptation:**
- Technical audience: Focus on Sacred Trinity, architecture
- Business audience: Focus on ROI, enterprise benefits
- Impact audience: Focus on community ownership, regenerative model
- General audience: Balance all aspects

### Customization Guide

**Replace these with your details:**
- [Your Name] - Your actual name

- Your personal story with the products

### Visual Suggestions

**Colors:**
- Primary: Deep purple (#673AB7)
- Accent: Purple (#9C27B0)
- Background: White or very light gray
- Text: Dark gray or black

**Fonts:**
- Headings: Bold, sans-serif (Inter, Roboto, etc.)
- Body: Regular, sans-serif
- Code: Monospace (Fira Code, Source Code Pro)

**Images to add:**
- Product screenshots
- Architecture diagrams
- Team photos (if applicable)
- Project photos (Terra Lumina)

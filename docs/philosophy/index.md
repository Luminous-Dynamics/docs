## Why Philosophy Matters in Technology

### Most technology companies:

1. **Start with features** ("What can we build?")
2. **Find users** ("Who will use this?")
3. **Optimize for growth** ("How do we scale?")
4. **Extract maximum value** ("How do we exit?")

### We start differently:

1. **Start with values** ("What do we believe about human flourishing?")
2. **Design for consciousness** ("How should this serve wellbeing?")
3. **Build sustainably** ("What's viable long-term for all stakeholders?")
4. **Create regenerative systems** ("How does this create lasting value?")

**The difference:** Our philosophy comes *first*, not as an afterthought or marketing layer.
